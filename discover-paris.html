Discover Paris

Welcome to Paris — a city where art, history, and community shape every street. Beyond the Eiffel Tower and Louvre, locals share hidden cafés, riverside secrets, and culture-rich neighborhoods that make Paris unforgettable.

Hidden Gems Loved by Locals

  • Rue des Barres: A medieval pedestrian street in Le Marais with cozy cafés and charming architecture.
  • Square du Vert-Galant: A peaceful riverside park at the tip of Île de la Cité — perfect for sunset views.
  • Promenade Plantée: A raised garden walkway above Paris, quieter and greener than the High Line.

Local Favorites

Parisians treasure small experiences — morning markets, vintage shopping streets, tucked-away bakeries, and peaceful spots by the Seine. These local picks bring you closer to the heart of Parisian culture.

  • Le Marché des Enfants Rouges: Oldest covered market with food stalls offering tastes from around the world.
  • Butte-aux-Cailles: A lesser-known artistic neighborhood filled with murals and indie shops.

Food & Café Recommendations

  • Café de Flore: Historic café ideal for people-watching and classic Parisian breakfasts.
  • Du Pain et des Idées: A beloved bakery known for its pistachio chocolate escargot pastry.
  • Pink Mamma: A colorful Italian hotspot for locals and travelers alike.

Weekly Activities

Every week, locals enjoy unique cultural events — here are a few to explore:

  • Seine River Pop-Up Concerts — Free open-air music events during spring and summer.
  • Le Marais Art Walks — Independent galleries open doors for evening community visits.
  • Bastille Food Market Sundays — A vibrant street market with fresh produce and regional treats.
