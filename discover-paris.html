Discover Paris

Paris is a city of artistry, culture, and quiet moments waiting to be discovered. Go beyond the Eiffel Tower and explore local cafés, riverside strolls, and hidden gems.

Hidden Gems Loved by Locals

  • Rue des Barres: Narrow medieval pedestrian street with cozy cafés.
  • Square du Vert-Galant: Peaceful riverside sunset spot.
  • Promenade Plantée: A raised garden walkway above the city.

Local Favorites

Experience Paris the way locals do — through bakeries, markets, and creative neighborhoods.

  • Le Marché des Enfants Rouges: Paris’s oldest covered market.
  • Butte-aux-Cailles: Artistic neighborhood filled with murals.

Food & Café Recommendations

  • Café de Flore: A classic Parisian breakfast spot.
  • Du Pain et des Idées: Beloved bakery known for pistachio escargot pastry.
  • Pink Mamma: A beautiful, cozy Italian restaurant loved by locals.

Weekly Activities

  • Seine Pop-Up Concerts: Free open-air music near the river.
  • Le Marais Art Walk: Evening gallery visits.
  • Bastille Sunday Market: Fresh pastries & local produce.
