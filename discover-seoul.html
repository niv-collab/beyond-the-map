Discover Seoul

Seoul blends futuristic energy with deep-rooted tradition. From neon-lit districts to peaceful hanok alleys, the city offers rich culture and warm community stories.

Hidden Gems Loved by Locals

  • Ikseon-dong Hanok Village: Narrow alleys filled with cafés and indie boutiques.
  • Seonyudo Park: A peaceful island park along the Han River.
  • Ihwa Mural Village: A hillside neighborhood covered in colorful murals.

Local Favorites

Seoulites enjoy simple pleasures — markets, scenic walks, tea houses, and night festivals.

  • Naksan Park: A stunning night-view hiking spot.
  • Hongdae Free Market: Handmade art and cultural events by local artists.
  • Hanok Tea Houses: Traditional tea spaces offering quiet escapes.

Food & Café Recommendations

  • Gwangjang Market: Iconic street food including bindaetteok and noodles.
  • Stylenanda Pink Pool Café: Aesthetic photo spots and trendy drinks.
  • Isaac Toast: A beloved breakfast favorite with sweet + savory combos.

Weekly Activities

  • Banpo Rainbow Fountain: A colorful water show on the Han River.
  • Dongdaemun Night Market: Late-night food stalls, shopping, and entertainment.
  • Weekend Han River Rides: Biking, picnics, and outdoor activities along the river.
