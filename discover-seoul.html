Discover Seoul

Seoul is a unique blend of futuristic energy and deep tradition. From neon-lit districts to peaceful hanok villages, every corner offers a mix of culture, history, and community stories shared by locals.

Hidden Gems Loved by Locals

  • Ikseon-dong Hanok Village: A cozy maze of cafés and boutiques inside restored hanok homes.
  • Seonyudo Park: A peaceful island park along the Han River with gardens and walkways.
  • Ihwa Mural Village: A hillside community filled with colorful murals and street art.

Local Favorites

Seoul’s charm lies in its contrasts — high-tech convenience surrounded by comforting traditions. Locals enjoy night markets, scenic hikes, and quiet tea houses hidden between skyscrapers.

  • Naksan Park: A scenic hiking spot with panoramic night views of Seoul.
  • Hongdae Free Market: A weekend art fair where local artists sell handmade crafts.
  • Hanok Tea Houses: Traditional tea spaces offering calm escapes from city life.

Food & Café Recommendations

  • Gwangjang Market: Famous for bindaetteok, tteokbokki, and endless street food stalls.
  • Stylenanda Pink Pool Café: An aesthetic café loved by influencers and locals.
  • Isaac Toast: A Korean breakfast favorite with sweet-and-salty toast combos.

Weekly Activities

Explore what's happening in Seoul this week:

  • Banpo Rainbow Fountain Show: A colorful water and light show along the Han River.
  • Dongdaemun Night Market: Late-night shopping and food vendors open past midnight.
  • Weekend Han River Bike Rides: Locals gather to bike, picnic, and enjoy river views.
