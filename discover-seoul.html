Discover Seoul

Seoul is where tradition and innovation live side by side. Explore vibrant neighborhoods, cozy tea houses, and creative cultural streets.

Hidden Gems Loved by Locals

  • Ikseon-dong Hanok Village: Traditional homes transformed into cafés and boutiques.
  • Seonyudo Park: A modern eco-park on an island in the Han River.
  • Ihwa Mural Village: A hillside community covered in art murals.

Local Favorites

Locals love balancing café culture with nature, hidden alleys, and artsy neighborhoods.

  • Naksan Park: Popular for night hikes and scenic city views.
  • Hongdae Free Market: A weekend art fair showcasing local talent.
  • Hanok Tea Houses: Traditional spots for calm, intimate gatherings.

Food & Café Recommendations

  • Gwangjang Market: Street food paradise: bindaetteok, noodles & more.
  • Stylenanda Pink Pool Café: Trendy, aesthetic café popular with visitors.
  • Isaac Toast: Sweet and savory Korean breakfast classic.

Weekly Activities

  • Banpo Rainbow Fountain: Colorful nighttime water show.
  • Dongdaemun Night Market: Midnight food stalls & shopping.
  • Han River Weekends: Biking, picnics, concerts, and more.
